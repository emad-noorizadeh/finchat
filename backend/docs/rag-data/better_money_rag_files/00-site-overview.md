## About Better Money Habits

Better Money Habits is Bank of America's free financial education platform, launched in 2013. It offers over 300 articles, videos, tools, and resources covering personal finance topics. The platform aims to support economic opportunity for individuals and communities by providing accessible financial literacy content.

The site features volunteer "Better Money Habits Champions" — Bank of America employees who deliver in-person financial guidance in communities across the country.

## Main Topic Categories

The site is organized into the following major sections:

1. **Saving & Budgeting** — Budgeting plans, saving strategies, emergency funds, spending analysis tools
2. **Credit** — Credit cards, credit scores, building credit, choosing credit cards
3. **Debt** — Paying off credit card debt, student loans, debt strategies
4. **Personal Banking** — Bank accounts, fees, checking vs savings, CDs, debit cards
5. **Homeownership** — Home buying process, closing costs, home equity, real estate terms
6. **Privacy & Security** — Identity theft, online security, scam protection
7. **Investing & Retirement** — 401(k), IRA, asset allocation, building wealth, retirement planning
8. **Auto** — Car buying, electric vehicles, auto costs
9. **Taxes & Income** — Tax preparation, employee benefits, career finance
10. **College** — Student loans, FAFSA, saving for college
