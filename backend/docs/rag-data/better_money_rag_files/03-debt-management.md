## Paying Off Credit Card Debt Faster

**Source URL:** https://bettermoneyhabits.bankofamerica.com/en/debt/how-to-pay-off-credit-card-debt-fast
**Read Time:** Article

### Key Strategies

Tackling credit card debt requires developing a plan and sticking to it. Common approaches include:

- **Avalanche method** — Focus payments on the card with the highest interest rate first while making minimum payments on others. This minimizes total interest paid over time.
- **Snowball method** — Focus on paying off the smallest balance first for a psychological win, then roll that payment into the next smallest balance.
- **Balance transfer** — Move high-interest balances to a card with a lower or 0% introductory APR. Be aware of balance transfer fees and the rate after the promotional period ends.
- **Pay more than the minimum** — Even small amounts above the minimum payment can significantly reduce interest costs and time to payoff.

## Strategies for Paying Down Debts

**Source URL:** https://bettermoneyhabits.bankofamerica.com/en/debt/strategies-for-paying-debts

### General Debt Reduction Approaches
- List all debts with balances, interest rates, and minimum payments
- Choose a repayment strategy (avalanche or snowball)
- Allocate extra funds beyond minimums toward targeted debt
- Consider debt consolidation to simplify payments
- Avoid adding new debt while paying down existing obligations
- Automate payments to prevent missed due dates

## Getting Emergency Cash

**Source URL:** https://bettermoneyhabits.bankofamerica.com/en/debt/how-to-get-emergency-money
**Read Time:** 7 minutes

### Options When You Need Cash Quickly
- Draw from an emergency savings fund (if one exists)
- Negotiate payment plans with creditors
- Look into personal loans from banks or credit unions
- Consider borrowing from retirement accounts (with caution — understand penalties and tax implications)
- Explore community assistance programs and non-profit resources
- Sell unneeded items
- Take on temporary or gig work

### What to Avoid
- High-interest payday loans
- Predatory lending products
- Excessive credit card cash advances

## Tackling Financial Stress

**Source URL:** https://bettermoneyhabits.bankofamerica.com/en/debt/how-to-overcome-financial-problems

### Six Approaches to Financial Stress
1. Acknowledge the stress and its sources
2. Create a realistic budget that accounts for current obligations
3. Prioritize high-interest debt repayment
4. Build even a small emergency fund to create a buffer
5. Seek free financial counseling or resources
6. Focus on progress, not perfection — small steps add up

## Student Loan Repayment

**Source URL:** https://bettermoneyhabits.bankofamerica.com/en/college?subTopicId=paying-back-student-loans

### Key Considerations
- Understand repayment plan options (standard, graduated, income-driven)
- Look into employer student loan repayment benefits
- Consider whether refinancing makes sense based on current rates
- Explore loan forgiveness programs for qualifying professions
- Avoid defaulting — contact loan servicer if struggling to make payments
